Who I am

I'm a web developer who loves working with new technologies and building interesting web projects.

My journey to coding is a bit different than usual. I tried to learn two professions before programming, architecture and philosophy, I have spent one and two years on them, respectively, later I liked more, but still, it was not a thing, which I wanted to do all my life, so I decided to take an academic year and work at a restaurant to support myself financially and the same time to think about my future.

I was working there more than a year and then 2020 came, so like many other people I also had plenty of free time and decided to try coding, which later proved, was one of the my best decisions, because I found to learn to program very funny and joyful, it was not like previous experiences with other professions. So I stuck with it and after one year I started to work in redberry as a junior web developer.

At this moment I am working with the following stacks:
backend: PHP, Laravel / fronted: Javascript, React
But if your project requires other skills and languages, I would be glad to take up new challenges and learn new interesting stuff.
